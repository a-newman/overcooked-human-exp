The purpose of this study is to test how well different AI agents can work with people. You'll play a version of the video game Overcooked with some agents, and we'll measure how well you do as a team.


Task Overview

The task will take approximately 9 minutes. You will be paid $2 plus a bonus of $0.01 per soup delivered with a partner.


Here is how the task will go:

  1. First, you will answer a couple of questions about yourself.
  2. Then, you'll read the Overcooked instructions.
  3. Then, you will play a "practice" round of Overcooked by yourself to get familiar with the game controls.
  4. Then, you will play the game for real with a series of AI partners. Try to get the best score you can with each partner. After playing with each partner, we'll ask some questions about your experience playing with them.

Do your best on the game and have fun!
