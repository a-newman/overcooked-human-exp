Movement and interactions

You can move up, down, left, and right using the arrow keys, and interact with objects using the spacebar.

You can interact with objects by facing them and pressing spacebar.


Making a Soup

The goal of the game is to serve as many onion soups as possible. An onion soup contains three onions. Click through the slides below to see how to serve a soup.

Score

Your goal is to serve as many soups as you can before the game ends!
