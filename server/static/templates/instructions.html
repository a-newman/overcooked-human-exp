Movement and interactions

You can move up, down, left, and right using the arrow keys, and interact with objects using the spacebar.

You can interact with objects by facing them and pressing spacebar.


Making a Soup

The goal of the game is to serve as many onion soups as possible. An onion soup contains three onions. Here's how you make one.

First, use the arrow keys to move to an onion dispenser:

Press the space bar to pick up an onion.

Use the arrow keys to walk to the stove.

Press the space bar to put the onion in the stove.

Once you put three onions in the pot, the soup will automatically start cooking. The soup will take 20 seconds to cook. A countdown timer over the soup will show how much time is left for it to cook.

While the soup is cooking, walk over and pick up a dish.

Bring the dish to the stove. Once the soup is done cooking, press the space bar to put the soup into the dish.

Deliver the soup by bringing it to the serving area and pressing the space bar.

Score

When a soup is served at the serving counter, you will receive a point.

Your goal is to serve as many soups as you can before the game ends!
