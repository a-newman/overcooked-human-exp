Debrief

Thank you for participating in our experiment! The purpose of our study is to evaluate how well different collaborative AI agents can coordinate with humans. In particular, we are testing a new kind of agent that is capable of adapting to different partners, including people. In order to evaluate different agents, we are having them work together in human-agent teams. We are collecting data about the performance of those teams, including performance (score) on the game and people's subjective experiences coordinating with their partner.

In this study, you collaborated with an agent partner. Data from this experiment will be used to evaluate how well your partner collaborates with humans.

Your participation will help us design agents that are better at assisting and collaborating with humans!
